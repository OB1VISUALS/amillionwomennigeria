NIGERIA STANDS AT A CRITICAL MOMENT

The Church is rising to pray, fast, and contend for the destiny of our nation.

In this defining hour for Nigeria, we are not gathering for another conference. We are answering an urgent call — to humble ourselves before the Lord, to fast, to pray, and to stand in the gap for our land.

Like Esther and Mordecai in their generation, we have been positioned "for such a time as this" (Esther 4:14). The Church must arise — not in fear, but in faith; not in division, but in unity; not in silence, but in fervent, effectual prayer.

This Solemn Assembly is our collective response. Across every state, across the FCT, across the diaspora — we will lift our voices as one, pleading the Blood of Jesus over Nigeria and proclaiming hope over every community.

One nation. One cry. His Blood settles it.
